OPEN CLOSED Principle  /  Principio Abierto/Cerrado

States: "Class should be Open for EXTENSIBILITY Closed for Modifications"

		"La noción de que las “entidades de software … 
			deben estar abiertas para su extensión, 
			pero cerradas para su modificación”



You are not allowed to make changes to the class once it's been moved into production.

Only Exception to the rule: Bug Fixing
